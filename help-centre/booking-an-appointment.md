# Booking an Appointment

We make it easy to book an appointment for your pet. You can book online, by phone, or by visiting us in person.

## Online Booking

Visit our website and click **Book an Appointment**. Select your pet, choose the type of visit, and pick a date and time that works for you. You'll receive a confirmation email once your booking is complete.

## By Phone

Call us at (416) 555-0100 during clinic hours. Our team will help you find the right appointment type and get you scheduled.

## Clinic Hours

- Monday to Friday: 8:00am – 7:00pm
- Saturday: 9:00am – 4:00pm
- Sunday: Closed

## New Patients

If this is your pet's first visit, please arrive 10 minutes early to complete a new patient form. You can also download and complete this form from our website before your appointment.

## Questions?

Contact us at hello@pawsclinic.com or call (416) 555-0100.
