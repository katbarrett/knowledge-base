# Payment and Insurance

We believe every pet deserves great care. Here's everything you need to know about payment and pet insurance at our clinic.

## Payment Methods

We accept the following forms of payment:

- Visa, Mastercard, and American Express
- Debit
- Cash
- E-transfer (payable to billing@pawsclinic.com)

Payment is due at the time of service.

## Estimates

For any procedure or treatment plan, we are happy to provide a written estimate in advance. Please ask our team before your appointment or at check-in.

## Pet Insurance

We strongly recommend pet insurance — unexpected illness or injury can result in significant costs, and insurance gives you the freedom to make decisions based on what's best for your pet rather than what you can afford in the moment.

We work with all major Canadian pet insurance providers, including Trupanion, Petplan, and Intact Pet Insurance. We can submit claims directly on your behalf for providers that support direct billing.

**Important:** Most pet insurance policies do not cover pre-existing conditions, so enrolling your pet while they are young and healthy is the best approach.

## Financial Hardship

If you are experiencing financial difficulty, please speak with our client care team. We will do our best to discuss available options, including payment plans for larger treatment costs.

## Questions About Your Invoice

If you have any questions about a charge on your invoice, contact us at billing@pawsclinic.com or call (416) 555-0100.
