# Cancellation Policy

We understand that life happens. If you need to cancel or reschedule, we ask that you give us as much notice as possible so we can offer your spot to another patient.

## How to Cancel

You can cancel or reschedule by calling us at (416) 555-0100 or by replying to your appointment confirmation email.

## Notice Requirements

- **General appointments:** Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance.
- **Specialist or surgical appointments:** Please cancel at least 48 hours in advance.

## Late Cancellations and No-Shows

Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice, or missed appointments without notice, may be subject to a $50 cancellation fee. This fee helps us manage our schedule and ensure all patients are seen in a timely manner.

## Emergencies

We understand that genuine emergencies happen. If you missed your appointment due to an emergency, please contact us and we will waive the fee at our discretion.
